The Duo Advantage: Two Levels of Protection

Neurolux Duo glasses feature two specialized FL-41 tint levels to provide optimal protection in every environment

Duo Indoor

A lighter tint specifically formulated for indoor environments with artificial lighting.

  • Ideal for offices, schools, and home environments
  • Optimized for fluorescent and LED lighting
  • Maintains natural color perception
  • Reduces screen glare without darkening vision
  • Perfect for extended wear during daily activities

Duo Outdoor

A darker tint designed for bright outdoor conditions and intense light exposure.

  • Enhanced protection against sunlight and glare
  • Filters intense light while maintaining visual clarity
  • Ideal for driving, outdoor activities, and bright days
  • Provides superior protection for highly sensitive patients
  • Can be worn over prescription glasses

Why Two Levels Matter

Traditional single-tint solutions force patients to compromise between indoor and outdoor effectiveness. The Neurolux Duo system provides:

  • Customized protection for different lighting environments
  • Optimal effectiveness in all conditions without compromise
  • Versatility for patients with varying sensitivity levels
  • Improved compliance through situation-specific solutions

How Neurolux Duo Works

1

Targeted Light Filtering

FL-41 lenses specifically block the 480-520nm blue-green spectrum that triggers photophobia and migraines.

2

Neural Pathway Modulation

By reducing stimulation of ipRGC cells, Neurolux glasses decrease activation of trigeminal pain pathways.

3

Preventative Protection

Worn proactively before symptom onset, they create a protective barrier against environmental triggers.
